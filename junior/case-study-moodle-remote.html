The Challenge: Structural Inefficiency

I led the Digital Transformation of the IELTS course, migrating 500+ assets into a unified Moodle environment, optimized for Remote Autonomy and mobile-first learning.

Pioneered the implementation of "Asymptotic Rubrics" strictly mapped to international standards, transforming qualitative feedback into a quantitative data pipeline.
