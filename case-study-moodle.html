🏛️ Project Executive Summary: MOODLE ECOSYSTEM

The Challenge: Scaling Qualitative Rigor

Traditional language training often struggles with subjective grading bias and slow feedback loops when scaling to hundreds of assets. We needed a system that maintained high pedagogical rigor while automating the operational core for a global workforce.

The Architecture: A Hybrid SCORM-Moodle Hub

I engineered a scalable learning ecosystem by architectural integration of Articulate Rise micro-modules into Moodle 4.x.

Pioneered the implementation of "Asymptotic Rubrics" strictly mapped to international standards, transforming qualitative feedback into a quantitative data pipeline.

Strategic Impact: Evidence-Based Pedagogy

Standardized Excellence: Eliminated assessment subjectivity by implementing 100% rubric-based grading, ensuring consistency across all faculty branches.

Data-Driven Remediation: Used Moodle’s advanced Gradebook reporting to identify struggling learners 3x faster than traditional paper-based systems.

Senior Architect’s Takeaway: This project proves my ability to lead Pedagogical Engineering at scale, turning complex, subjective learning requirements into standardized, high-volume automated systems.

Pedagogical Architecture

Designed for academic rigor, this course utilizes a scaffolded learning pathway. Each module integrates instructional content via Articulate Rise blocks directly embedded into Moodle, followed by weighted assessments to ensure cumulative knowledge gain.

Structured Curriculum: Logic-gated topics ensuring a linear learning journey.

Precision Grading & Feedback

To eliminate subjective bias in writing assessments, I engineered complex Advanced Grading Rubrics aligned with official IELTS Band Descriptors (Task Response, Cohesion, Lexical Resource, Grammatical Range).

Advanced Rubric: Automated criterion-based marking for consistent feedback.

Data-Driven Monitoring

L&D managers benefit from real-time Activity Completion Tracking and a centralized Gradebook, providing 100% visibility into student success rates and critical bottlenecks.

Administrative Backbone

Behind the scenes, I optimized the LMS settings for scalability, including automated enrolment methods and conditional access restricted by formative assessment scores.
