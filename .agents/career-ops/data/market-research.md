## KEY MARKET INSIGHTS

### Remote L&D Market (2025-2026)
- Articulate, Crossover, LearningMate, Chegg, Edmentum — active remote employers
- **APAC timezone roles** available through companies hiring in Philippines, Australia, Singapore
- Vietnam-based candidates can apply for most "Worldwide Remote" roles but may face salary adjustment
- **Top skills in demand**: SCORM, xAPI, Articulate 360, Moodle, ADDIE, SAM, behavioral assessment

### Customer Success EdTech Market
- Former educators/teachers are valued in CS-EdTech — understanding the user persona is a competitive advantage
- Core CS skills needed: CRM (Salesforce/HubSpot), retention metrics (NRR, churn), onboarding, upsell
- **Gap for Phi**: No B2B SaaS sales experience — better fit for Implementation/Onboarding roles than sales-heavy CSM
- Bilingual English/Vietnamese is an asset for APAC regional roles

### Ghost Job Warning Signs
- Job posted 60+ days ago
- Generic description with no specific tools named
- Apply button redirects to generic careers page
- Company had recent layoffs in same department
