**Tiêu đề:** From Pedagogy to Architecture: Why I bridge the gap with code. 🏗️🚀

**Nội dung:**
For 10 years, I’ve been building learning experiences. But for a long time, I didn't realize that what I was doing had a name: Instructional Design.

My journey started in the classroom, but it quickly moved into the "backend" of learning. I realized that the best pedagogical theory fails if the technical infrastructure can't scale.

Why I code as an ID: Most industry tools have ceilings. To break them, I use JavaScript ES6 to build high-fidelity simulators (like my recent Deadline Crisis project) and custom LMS data pipelines. I believe an Architect shouldn't just draw the blueprints—they should know how the bricks are laid.

What I’m building now: Systems that track real behavioral change, not just "click-next" completion rates. Systems that turn learner data into actionable insights for organizations.

If you're building the future of EdTech or Corporate L&D and need someone who speaks both "Pedagogy" and "Infrastructure"—let’s connect.
