# MASTER RESUME GUIDELINES (Premium Standard)
*Updated: April 5, 2026*

## I. CORE PRINCIPLES (The 15 Laws)
1.  **Length (1–3 Pages)**: Aim for 1 page if experience < 5 years. Cut what doesn’t help you get this role.
2.  **Sharp Professional Summary**: Highlight your years of experience, industry, key skills, and impact.
3.  **Tailoring (70% Match)**: Mirror 3–5 keywords from the Job Description (JD).
4.  **Achievements Over Responsibilities**: Use results to show what changed because of you — not just what you were assigned.
5.  **Action Verbs**: Start each bullet point with a powerful action verb (See Section IV).
6.  **Quantify Wherever Possible**: Show scale, scope, or results (e.g., “Reduced churn by 18%” or “Managed $200K budget”).
7.  **Highlight Recent/Relevant Experience First**: Condense or cut older or unrelated roles. Prioritize what supports this application.
8.  **Show Career Progression**: Include promotions, added responsibilities, or team growth to signal momentum.
9.  **Standard Job Titles**: If your past titles are confusing, adjust them to match market terms.
10. **Focused Tech/Certs**: Keep it focused — don’t list every course you’ve ever taken.
11. **Avoid Jargon**: Translate company-specific terms into language the recruiter will understand.
12. **Professional Profile**: Use professional email + LinkedIn profile link. No nicknames.
13. **Proofread Carefully**: Typos = instant red flag. Use tools like Grammarly or get a second pair of eyes.
14. **Skip Irrelevant Detail**: Skip hobbies, full address, references, or anything that wastes space.
15. **Save & Send as PDF**: Prevents formatting breaks. Format: `Resume_FirstName_LastName.pdf`.

## II. SUMMARY SECTION STRUCTURE
*   **Bird’s Eye View**: Provide an high-level overview of your profile.
*   **Bullet 1 (The Hook)**: One sentence: **Years of exp + Roles/Fields + Types of companies**.
*   **Bullets 2-4 (Impact)**: 2–4 bullets showcasing specific, measurable results related to role, company, or industry. 
*   **Total Count**: Aim for **3–5 bullets** in total.

## III. PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE FORMATTING
*   **Header**: `Job Title | Dates (e.g. Jan 2018 – May 2024) | Company | Location`
*   **Company Summary (Context)**: Max 3 lines describing: *What they do, for whom, their geographical reach, number of employees, and approx. annual revenue.*
*   **Bullet Guidelines (5–7 bullets per role)**:
    *   Include team size managed and reporting line.
    *   Tie responsibility or result to a metric related to a wider business goal.
    *   Define scope/reach (State-wide, country-wide, or International).
    *   Include budget managed or size of market.
    *   Highlight metrics you tracked or improved within a specific timeframe.
    *   Include specific KPIs achieved (1–2 bullets).

## IV. CATEGORIZED ACTION VERBS
*   **Leadership/Ownership**: Led, Directed, Managed, Oversaw, Chaired, Coordinated.
*   **Impact/Results**: Increased, Reduced, Improved, Accelerated, Boosted, Delivered.
*   **Strategy/Planning**: Developed, Designed, Implemented, Launched, Initiated, Formulated.
*   **Analysis/Insight**: Analyzed, Audited, Evaluated, Assessed, Identified, Investigated.
*   **Execution/Delivery**: Executed, Built, Created, Produced, Engineered, Deployed.
*   **Communication**: Presented, Advised, Negotiated, Facilitated, Briefed, Persuaded.
*   **Collaboration**: Collaborated, Supported, Partnered, Contributed, Consulted, Liaised.
