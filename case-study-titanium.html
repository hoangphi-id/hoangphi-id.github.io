THE DEADLINE CRISIS

Adaptive Learning Architecture & Behavioral Simulation

Tools: Articulate Storyline 360, JavaScript ES6, Google Apps Script, Data Webhooks.

1. THE PROBLEM

In high-stakes corporate environments, communication during a "Project Crisis" is often the difference between success and total failure. Standard e-learning fails to simulate the cumulative psychological impact of communication choices.

"Traditional modules tell you what to do. The Titanium Simulator makes you feel the consequences of your choices."

2. DATA ARCHITECTURE

To bypass standard SCORM limitations and achieve real-time automation, I engineered a seamless external pipeline.

The Webhook Processor: A custom-built Google Apps Script acting as the simulator's "Brain".

3. THE BEHAVIORAL JOURNEY

The learner navigates through three critical stages: Negotiation, Strategy Email, and Team Updates. Each decision affects a global TrustScore variable.

graph TD Start((Crisis Starts)) --> S1[Stage 1: Negotiation] S1 --> S1_A[Option A: Yes-man] S1 --> S1_B[Option B: Passive-Aggressive] S1 --> S1_C[Option C: Proactive Negotiator] S1_A & S1_B & S1_C --> S2[Stage 2: Formal Email] S2 --> S3[Stage 3: Team Update] S3 --> End[Final Achievement Dashboard] style S1_C fill:#00f2ff,stroke:#fff,color:#000 style End fill:#00f2ff,stroke:#fff,color:#000
Variable-based branching logic: Every decision is tracked and aggregated for final reporting.

The simulator features a dynamic dashboard that changes its visual state (Glow Effects) based on the user's real-time performance.

5. MEASURABLE RESULTS

Total transparency for L&D Managers. Every decision is logged into the Behavioral Data Warehouse, allowing for post-simulation coaching and gap analysis.

Live Data Storage: Confirming 100% data sync across the simulation environment.
