# Market Intel: Global Education Standards (2025-2026)

This document summarizes the core standards governing the "Build Phía Sau" (Technical ID/Curriculum) roles in the global remote market.

## 1. UNESCO AI Competency Framework (2026)
**Concept**: A human-centered approach to AI in education. It prioritizes ethics and inclusivity over technical automation.

### Key Dimensions for Curriculum Developers:
- **Human-Centered Mindset**: AI must enhance human capacity, not replace it. Curricula should be designed for student needs and social responsibility.
- **AI Pedagogy**: Integrating AI into lesson planning and assessment to improve outcomes, with a focus on "Creative Integration" (innovating new teaching methods).
- **Ethics & Privacy**: Mandatory adherence to data privacy, security, and the mitigation of algorithmic bias.

---

## 2. IB Digital Learning Principles (2026)
**Concept**: Inquiry-based, learner-centered education. The IB is the "gold standard" for international schools.

### Core Mandates for Developers:
- **Inquiry-Driven AI**: AI tools must *deepen* critical thinking, not provide shortcuts. Developers must design activities that require process-based evidence.
- **Approaches to Teaching and Learning (ATL)**: Every digital module must explicitly support ATL skills (Research, Communication, Self-management, Social, Thinking).
- **Safe & Transparent**: Data privacy is non-negotiable. High-stakes decisions (like grading) must retain human oversight.
- **Conceptual Understanding**: Moving beyond content delivery to focus on "Key Concepts" (e.g., Change, Systems, Perspective).
