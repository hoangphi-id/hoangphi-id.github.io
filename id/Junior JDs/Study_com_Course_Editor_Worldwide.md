# Job Description: Course Editor / Curriculum Designer - Study.com

**Location:** Worldwide (Remote / Freelance)
**Source:** Study.com / Himalayas.app (April 2026)
**Type:** Contract / Freelance / Flexible

## Position Overview
Study.com is looking for passionate Course Editors and Curriculum Designers to join our freelance network. This role is 100% remote and allows you to work whenever and wherever you want. You will be responsible for maintaining and enhancing the quality of college-level courses that help millions of students worldwide.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Content Review:** Evaluate and edit existing course structures, lesson materials, and video scripts for pedagogical effectiveness.
- **Curriculum Alignment:** Ensure course objectives align with established academic standards and industry certifications.
- **Quality Assurance:** Refine assignment prompts, rubrics, and multiple-choice assessment questions for accuracy and clarity.
- **Organization:** Organize course sections to ensure a logical and engaging flow for learners.

## Qualifications & Requirements
- **Education:** Master’s degree or higher in a specific subject area (e.g., Business, IT, Humanities, Education) is highly preferred.
- **Experience:** Previous teaching experience at the college or university level, or experience in academic course development/revision.
- **Skills:** Strong time management and the ability to work independently. Receptiveness to feedback and collaboration with in-house designers.
- **Technical:** Comfort using online project management and course authoring systems.

## Freelance Benefits
- **Full Flexibility:** No minimum or maximum hours. Choose your own projects and schedule.
- **Reliable Payments:** Twice-monthly payments via automated invoicing.
- **Support:** Access to a dedicated in-house team to help with onboarding and project questions.
- **Impact:** Contribute to courses used by millions of students globally.
