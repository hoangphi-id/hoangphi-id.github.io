# Job Description: Instructional Designer (Contract) - Lifted

**Location:** Worldwide (Remote)
**Source:** Remotive / Upwork (April 2026)
**Type:** Contract / Part-time

## Position Overview
Lifted is seeking a highly skilled and creative Instructional Designer to join our remote-first, global team. In this role, you will act as a strategic learning architect, blending pedagogical expertise, user experience (UX) design, and modern technology to create impactful, engaging, and accessible learning experiences for a diverse, worldwide audience. You will translate complex product functionality into clear, actionable guidance for enterprise customers.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Needs Analysis & Strategy:** Conduct thorough needs assessments to identify performance gaps and define clear, measurable learning objectives.
- **Course Design & Development:** Design and develop end-to-end learning experiences (eLearning, instructor-led, and blended formats). Create storyboards, scripts, and multimedia content.
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Partner effectively with subject matter experts (SMEs), stakeholders, and global team members.
- **Technical Implementation:** Leverage modern eLearning authoring tools (e.g., Articulate 360, Adobe Captivate, Camtasia) and Learning Management Systems (LMS).
- **Data-Informed Improvement:** Evaluate the effectiveness of learning solutions using data analytics and iterate based on feedback.
- **Inclusive Design:** Apply Universal Design for Learning (UDL) principles and adhere to accessibility standards (WCAG).

## Qualifications & Essential Skills
- **Education:** Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Instructional Design, Educational Technology, or related field.
- **Experience:** Typically 3–6+ years in instructional design or eLearning development. (Junior/Mid level friendly if tools are mastered).
- **Tools:** Advanced proficiency in Articulate Storyline/Rise, Adobe Captivate, or similar.
- **Global Mindset:** Sensitivity to cultural nuances and Delivering training across different geographical regions and time zones.

## Why Join (Remote Worldwide)
- **Flexible Environment:** Work from anywhere in the world.
- **Innovation-Focused:** Engage with cutting-edge learning technologies, including AI-integrated design.
