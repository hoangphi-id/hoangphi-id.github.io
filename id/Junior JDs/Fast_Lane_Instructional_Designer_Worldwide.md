# Job Description: Instructional Designer for E-Learning - Fast Lane

**Location:** Worldwide (Remote)
**Source:** Fast Lane Group (flane.de)
**Type:** Full-time / Remote

## Position Overview
As an Instructional Designer for E-Learning at Fast Lane, you are responsible for creating engaging and effective online training content to meet the needs of our global IT training clients. You will work within a collaborative, dynamic team to transform complex technical concepts into accessible, high-impact learning materials for an international audience.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Content Development:** Design and develop innovative, interactive e-learning modules, courses, and resources aligned with instructional goals and industry best practices.
- **Collaboration:** Partner closely with technical Subject Matter Experts (SMEs), multimedia designers, and technical writers to integrate diverse media (graphics, animations, videos, simulations).
- **Storyboarding:** Create detailed storyboards, scripts, and outlines to guide the multimedia development process.
- **Methodology:** Utilize sound instructional design methodologies and adult learning principles to foster a learner-centered experience that promotes engagement, retention, and skill application.
- **Quality Assurance:** Conduct regular reviews, revisions, and QA testing of content to ensure accuracy, technical functionality, and effectiveness.
- **Project Management:** Collaborate with project managers to ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget.

## Qualifications & Essential Skills
- **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Instructional Design, Education, Technology, or a related field (Master’s degree preferred).
- **Experience:** Proven experience (typically 3+ years) in instructional design for e-learning, with a portfolio showing high-fidelity interactive materials.
- **Tools:** Proficiency in industry-standard e-learning authoring tools (e.g., Articulate Storyline, Adobe Captivate, Rise 360) and Learning Management Systems (LMS).
- **Technical Curiosity:** Comfort working with technical content in the IT and software domain.
- **Communication:** Excellent written and verbal communication skills for a global, remote environment.

## Why Fast Lane?
- **Global Reach:** Contribute to training programs used by top-tier IT companies and professionals worldwide.
- **Remote Culture:** Fully remote position with a focus on collaborative technical innovation.
- **Stability:** Part of an established global leader in high-end IT training and consulting.
