# Job Description: Technical Instructional Designer - GitLab

**Location:** Worldwide (Remote / Distributed)
**Source:** GitLab Handbook (March 2026)
**Type:** Full-time / Remote-first

## Position Overview
The Technical Instructional Designer at GitLab is responsible for designing and developing engaging, effective technical learning solutions for customers, partners, and internal teams (Sales & Customer Success). This role focuses on helping global audiences achieve technical proficiency with the GitLab platform through scalable online delivery.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Design & Development:** Create experiential learning solutions using adult learning best practices and job/task analysis to drive behavior change.
- **Content Creation:** Produce and curate learning content using modern techniques and technology (scalable e-learning, interactive videos).
- **Stakeholder Management:** Build relationships with Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) and external vendors to ensure content accuracy and quality.
- **Authoring:** Utilize advanced authoring tools (Articulate 360, Adobe Captivate) to rapidly build and iterate on technical learning experiences.
- **Assessment:** Develop instructionally sound knowledge-based and performance-based assessments.
- **Measurement:** Evaluate business impact through learning metrics and performance data.
- **Product Mastery:** Maintain a deep working knowledge of GitLab’s functional and technological product areas.

## Requirements
- **Education:** Bachelor’s degree (required); Master’s degree in Instructional Design or Educational Technology (preferred).
- **Experience:** Progressive work experience in B2B SaaS training or technical instructional design.
- **Authoring Skills:** Proficiency with modern e-learning authoring suites (e.g., Articulate 360, Adobe Captivate).
- **Scalability:** Proven experience in designing and developing scalable, global online training.
- **Global Mindset:** Ability to design for diverse learning styles and international, distributed audiences.

## Why GitLab?
- **All-Remote:** Work from anywhere in the world as part of a 100% distributed team.
- **Transparency:** Open and transparent company culture and handbook.
- **Impact:** Scale learning for one of the world's leading DevSecOps platforms.
