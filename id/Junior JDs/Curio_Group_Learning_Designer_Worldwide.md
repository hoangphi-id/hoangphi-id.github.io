# Job Description: Learning Designer (Contract) - Curio Group

**Location:** Worldwide (Remote / Global)
**Source:** Curio.co / Expertini (April 2026)
**Type:** Contract / Remote-friendly

## Position Overview
Curio Group is a leading education consulting firm that partners with universities and organizations globally to design and deliver premium learning experiences. We are seeking a versatile Learning Designer to join our global network for remote contract opportunities. You will work on high-impact projects, from higher education degree programs to corporate technical training.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Learning Experience Design:** Design and develop end-to-end learning solutions (online, blended, and social learning) for diverse student and professional audiences.
- **SME Collaboration:** Facilitate workshops and consultations with Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) to extract knowledge and map it to effective learning outcomes.
- **Pedagogical Strategy:** Apply advanced instructional design frameworks (e.g., Constructivism, Backward Design) to ensure high-quality, research-backed learning.
- **Multimedia Integration:** Define the visual and interactive strategy for courses, collaborating with multimedia designers to produce high-end educational assets.
- **Assessment Design:** Create robust assessment strategies that effectively measure complex skill acquisition and knowledge retention.
- **Quality Assurance:** Lead the peer review and QA process to maintain the "Curio Standard" of excellence in educational delivery.

## Qualifications & Skills
- **Experience:** 3+ years of experience in learning design, ideally with exposure to both higher education and corporate sectors.
- **Educational Background:** Degree in Education, Instructional Design, or a related field.
- **Technical Skills:** Proficiency in major LMS platforms (Canvas, Moodle, Blackboard) and authoring tools (Articulate 360, H5P).
- **Consulting Mindset:** Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to act as a consultant to academic and business stakeholders.
- **Global Adaptability:** Experience working in multicultural, distributed teams with a focus on inclusive and accessible design.

## Why Curio?
- **Elite Projects:** Work with top-tier global universities and innovative edtech partners.
- **Global Network:** Connect with a world-class team of educational researchers, tech experts, and designers.
- **Flexibility:** Remote-first contract opportunities that allow for diverse project exposure.
