# Syllabus: The AI-Native English Educator (Pilot v1.0)

**Duration**: 1 Week (3 Units)
**Target**: English Teachers pivoting to Remote/Digital Learning.
**Focus**: IELTS Writing Task 2 & Moodle Workflows.

---

## 📅 Course Breakdown

### [Unit 1] The Socratic AI Tutor (Pedagogy + Framework)
*   **Goal**: Move from "AI corrected my grammar" to "AI helped me think".
*   **Concepts**: UNESCO Human-Centered AI, Scaffolding.
*   **Technical Asset**: **The Socratic Prompt Library (IELTS Writing Focus)**.
    *   How to build a prompt that asks probing questions based on the 4 IELTS criteria (TR, CC, LR, GRA).

### [Unit 2] The Async Feedback Loop (Remote Workflow)
*   **Goal**: Mastery of asynchronous communication to eliminate back-to-back meetings.
*   **Concepts**: Deep Work, Async-first Collaboration.
*   **Technical Asset**: **The Loom-to-Notion Workflow**.
    *   Proving "Remote Fluency" by creating status video templates and feedback logs.

### [Unit 3] Digital Compliance & LMS Scalability (Standards)
*   **Goal**: Designing lessons that are "Audited and Ready" for International Schools.
*   **Concepts**: IB ATL Skills, UNESCO Digital Literacy.
*   **Technical Asset**: **The Moodle Course Blueprint**.
    *   Mapping gating logic (Restrict access) and automated rubrics.
